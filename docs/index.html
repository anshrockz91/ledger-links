Free WordPress plugin

Ledger Links

Affiliate link cloaking for WordPress — with a free broken-link checker, honest click analytics, and CSV import/export. No upsell nags.

What it does

Turn long, ugly affiliate URLs into clean branded links (yoursite.com/go/best-laptop) that redirect visitors to the real product page — while tracking clicks and catching dead links before they cost you commission.

Free, forever

  • Unlimited cloaked links with 301/302/307 redirect types
  • Automatic broken-link checker, twice a day, plus a manual check-now button
  • Click analytics with bot-filtering and self-click exclusion on by default — the honest count, not the inflated one
  • Device targeting: send mobile visitors to a different URL than desktop
  • CSV import and export, no row limits
  • Zero in-admin upsell popups, ever

Pro (optional)

Geo-redirects, an autolinker for keyword-to-link automation, advanced analytics, and multi-site licensing for agencies.
